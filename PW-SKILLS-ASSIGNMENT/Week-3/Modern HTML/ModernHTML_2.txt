2. What are HTML Entities. List out 5 commonly used HTML entities.

Ans:-  Some characters are reserved in HTML.

If you use the less than (<) or greater than (>) signs in your text, the browser might mix them with tags.

Character entities are used to display reserved characters in HTML

1. &copy; - copyright - &#169;
	
2. &dollar; - Dollar sign - &#36;

3. &euro; - euro - &#8364;	

4. &amp; - Exclamation mark - &#33;

5. &lt; - Less than sign - &#60;

6. &gt; - Greater than sign - &#62;
