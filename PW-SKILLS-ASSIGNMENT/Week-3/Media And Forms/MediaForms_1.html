PW SKILL

Physics Wallah Private Limited (commonly known as Physics Wallah; or simply PW) is an Indian educational technology company headquartered in Noida, Uttar Pradesh.[1][2] It was founded by Alakh Pandey and Prateek Maheshwari in 2020. It became a unicorn in June 2022 after raising 1100 million at a valuation of 11.1 billion.[3]
